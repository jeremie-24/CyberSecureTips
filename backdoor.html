What Is It?

A backdoor can be introduced into your computer by a hacker. This malware lets the hacker spy on your activities, and it can even let him take control of your device. With total control over your computer, the hacker can steal your passwords and your credit card number. This control can also l use your computer remotely to make it seem like you are the one using it.


  1. The hacker sends a trapped file by email.
  2. The victim opens the file.
  3. The hacker takes control of the victim’s device.
  4. Hacking of the rights to delete, create and copy files.
