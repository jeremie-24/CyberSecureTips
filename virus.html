What Are Computer Viruses?

Computer viruses are malware that infects your computer. Malware is the common term used for any harmful program or malicious code. Malware can access your personal information on your computer or any kind of electronic device. Computer viruses spread across different devices like real-world viruses. There are many types of viruses like the Trojan horse virus and the worm virus. Some of them spread across the network and others are downloaded by the user without knowing it.
